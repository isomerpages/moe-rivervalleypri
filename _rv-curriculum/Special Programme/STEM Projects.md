<p>The RVPS Eco STEM Creators programme is designed to equip students with
emerging 21st-century competencies (e21CC) through a unique blend of STEM
education and sustainability. STEM education is an approach to learning
that integrates Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics.</p>
<p>Through this programme, students will explore real-world applications
and develop their resilience and 21st-century competencies such as critical
thinking, communication, global awareness and civic literacy. The programme
empowers students to take ownership of their learning, demonstrating how
sustainability and innovation can create a lasting impact on their school
and community.</p>

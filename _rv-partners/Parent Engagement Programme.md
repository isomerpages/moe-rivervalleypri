<h2>Growing Together: Parent Engagement at RVPS</h2>
<p>"When parents and educators work hand in hand, every child soars higher."</p>
<p></p>
<h4>Our Partnership Approach</h4>
<p>At River Valley Primary School, we believe that meaningful parent engagement
creates a powerful support system for our children's growth. Through our
structured Parent Engagement Programme (PEP@RVPS), we have created multiple
pathways for parents to be actively involved in their children's educational
journey.</p>
<h4>Stay Connected</h4>
<p>We keep our parent community well-informed through:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Regular updates from our Principal highlighting key initiatives and achievements</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Timely communications from form teachers about your child's progress</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Our vibrant bi-annual RV Heartbeat newsletter</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Digital platforms including Parents Gateway and our school website</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h3><strong>Join Our Learning Community</strong></h3>
<h4>Parent Education Series</h4>
<p>Throughout the year, we offer specially curated workshops and briefings
to help you support your child's learning:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Subject-specific workshops (Mathematics, Science, Languages)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Digital literacy and cyber-wellness seminars</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Educational landscape updates</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Primary 1 orientation programmes</p>
<p></p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4>Parent Support Group (PSG)</h4>
<p>Make a difference in our children's school experience by volunteering
with our dynamic PSG. Our parent volunteers contribute to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Engaging storytelling sessions</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Library programme support</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Festival celebrations</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Major school events</p>
</li>
</ul>
